About

Lunar Ray is a cooperation between two professional developers.

Their day job exposes them to the many positive and negative aspects of time and money constrained software developement. This exposure inspired them to apply them skills in a less constrained environment. Applying their ideals in an environment that allows as much space as possible for high quality. With their passion and belief in open source software they intend to write high quality open source software.

The idea of how this environment should look like has varied over the years. Since we both are happy in our professional careers, Lunar Ray is little more than a name for a hobby.

We acknowledge that most of our work comes from a dissatisfaction with something existing, believing we can provide an interesting new approach.
